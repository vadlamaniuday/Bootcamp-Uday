Design Review - Rohan B

TLDR Review :

The Weather Dashboard is a sleek and functional website for checking weather information. The layout is clean and intuitive, with a prominent search bar and well-organized weather details. The styling is modern and visually appealing, making the information easily readable.

1. Layout

Structure: The layout is clean and well-organized. The website is divided into clear sections for entering the city name and displaying the weather information. This separation helps users quickly understand and interact with the dashboard.

Search Bar: The search bar is prominently placed at the top, making it easy for users to input the city name and update the weather information.

Weather Display: The weather information is presented in a card format, which is visually appealing and easy to read. The card includes essential details such as temperature, weather condition, humidity, wind speed, pressure, visibility, and cloud cover.

2. Styling

Color Scheme: The use of a dark background with light text creates a modern and professional look. The color scheme is easy on the eyes and enhances readability.

Typography: The font choices are simple and readable. The use of different font sizes and weights helps in distinguishing between headings and body text.

Buttons and Icons: The buttons are well-designed and intuitive. The "UPDATE WEATHER" button is clearly labeled and stands out, making it easy for users to interact with the dashboard. The icons next to each weather detail add a visual element that enhances understanding.

3. Information Flow

Content Presentation: The information is presented in a clear and concise manner. Each weather detail is accompanied by an icon, making it easy for users to quickly understand the information.

Interactive Functionality: The interactive feature allows users to search for weather information in any city, making the site highly functional and user-friendly.

Navigation: The layout and design make it easy for users to navigate through the different sections of the website. The clear division between the search bar and the weather display helps users quickly find the information they need.
