Design Review - Zoya Haider

This is well-done. However, the absence of the 'zen garden' in Exercise 1 and can be benefitted with using appropriate color choices

Here is the review of each and every exercises along with the reason as to why
Exercise.No Topic Rating out of 10 Reason for Rating
1 Fonts 7.5

The content is good, so is styling and information is presentbut does not follow the guidelines of CSS Zen Garden Template.

2 Colors 9

Level 1 : This is good because the information is complementing from each sub exercises and the layout and styling is consistent all across
Level 2 : Actually very good. The color choice in dark mode is actually perfect and easy to the eye as well.
Level 3: The HSL is actually very good and provides all of the information . The gradients are good and is complimenting the background color and is aesthetically pleasing

Level 1: Actually very good along with image annotations. The UI/UX of Amazon/Flipkart comparison would've been benefitted with consistent styling.
