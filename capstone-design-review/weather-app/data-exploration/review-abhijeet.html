Design Review - Abhijeet

TLDR Review :

The website appears to be well-designed, functional, and user-friendly. It effectively presents a large amount of information in an organized and visually appealing manner.

1. Layout

Structure: The layout is clean and organized, with a clear division between the filter options on the left and the research paper cards on the right.

Navigation: The navigation is straightforward, with filters for authors, titles, and years, making it easy for users to find specific papers.

Spacing: There is ample spacing between the different sections and elements, which enhances readability.

Pagination: The pagination at the bottom left is well-placed and easy to use, allowing users to navigate through multiple pages of content.

2. Design

Functionality: The design is functional and user-friendly. The filter options and export button are prominently displayed, making it clear how users can interact with the site.

Consistency: The design elements are consistent across the page, which helps in maintaining a cohesive look and feel.

Accessibility: The design appears to be accessible, with clear text and contrasting colors that make it easy to read.

3. Styling

Color Scheme: The dark background with light text creates a modern and professional look. The use of a dark theme is visually appealing and reduces eye strain.

Typography: The font choices are clean and readable. The use of different font sizes and weights helps in distinguishing between headings and body text.

Icons and Buttons: The buttons and icons are well-designed and intuitive. The "Export as CSV" button is clearly labeled and stands out.

4. Content

Information Display: Each research paper card contains essential information such as authors, year, venue, and citations. This makes it easy for users to quickly scan and find relevant papers.

Detail Level: The level of detail provided for each paper is adequate for a quick overview. Users can get a sense of the paper's significance without being overwhelmed by too much information.

Citations: Including the number of citations for each paper is a useful feature, as it gives users an idea of the paper's impact and relevance.
