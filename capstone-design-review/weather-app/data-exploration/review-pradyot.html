Design Review - Pradyot

First Impressions

The styling and layout is consistent all across the exercises. Though this exercise would be benefitted with the presence of images and icons.

Here is the review of each and every exercises along with the reason as to why
Exercise.No Topic Rating out of 10 Reason for Rating
1 Fonts 9 One of the most consistent and visuallly pleasing submissions I've seen with respect to styling. The information structure flows well and the color and font choice is good.
2 Colors 8.5

Level 1 : The color Scheming card exercises are one of the best and informative exercises I've seen. It gives all of the information without additional work for the user.
Level 2 : The dark and light theme exercise is good, but the text in the color swatch exercises can be improved further.
Level 3: The gradient button exercise is good and easy to the eyes. The color choice of the button is not complementing the background color.

3 Icons 6.5 Level 1 : The use of icons are good and the color palette respects the background theme. NITPICK : The numbers are on the left, the content is in the middle of the page. That can be fixed
Level 2 : This is good, but if the information regarding which images are SVG and which are icons were provided, it would've been better
Level 3: There are no icons present in this.,
4 Images 7 Level 1 : Some context, with respect to the image properties would've been beneficial.
Level 2 : Provided all of the info as to what this exercise was actually supposed to be doing NITPICK : Could've been better if context regarding the properties are given
Level 3: Just an image. Additional context would've been better
5 Layouts and Spacing 8.5 Level 1 : One of the best medium clones I've seen with just HTML, CSS. Follows all of the spacing and margin guidelines.
Level 2 : This one is actually very good.Would've been benefitted if they added date as well so to know when the article was published
6 Design Principles and Redesign 6.5

Level 1 & 2: The content is strong and the styling is good, the information structure flows perfectly and the points are valid as well. The UI UX comparison is also good, styling of the tables are good and is told what is asked, good for me.
Level 3 : The Website redesign is good but can be improved in terms of images and spacing and layout
