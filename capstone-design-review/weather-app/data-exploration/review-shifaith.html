Design Review - Shifaith Ali

TLDR Review :

This website is a well-designed, functional, and user-friendly website for searching and filtering research papers. The layout is clean and organized, with a prominent search bar and intuitive filter options. The styling is professional and readable, though the authors' format could be improved. Overall, the site effectively presents a large amount of information in an accessible manner.

1. Layout

Structure: The layout is clean and well-organized. The search bar at the top is prominently placed, making it easy for users to start their search immediately.

Filters: The filter options on the left side are neatly arranged, allowing users to refine their search by abstract, authors, number of citations, references, and title. This makes the site highly functional and user-friendly.

Results Display: The research paper results are displayed in a clear and readable format on the right side. Each paper card contains essential information such as title, authors, year, abstract, citations, references, and venue.

2. Styling

Color Scheme: The use of a light background with dark text creates a professional and clean look. The color scheme is easy on the eyes and enhances readability.

Typography: The font choices are simple and readable. The use of different font sizes and weights helps in distinguishing between headings and body text.

Buttons and Icons: The buttons and icons are well-designed and intuitive. The "Export Data" button is clearly labeled and stands out, making it easy for users to export their search results.

3. Information Flow

Search Functionality: The search bar allows users to search across all fields, making it easy to find specific research papers. The filter options further enhance the search functionality by allowing users to refine their search results.

Detail Level: Each research paper card provides a good level of detail, including the title, authors, year, abstract, citations, references, and venue. This allows users to get a comprehensive overview of each paper without being overwhelmed by too much information.

Navigation: The layout and design make it easy for users to navigate through the search results and filters. The pagination at the bottom (not visible in the screenshot) would likely be well-placed and easy to use.

Nitpicking

Authors Format: The authors are displayed in an array format (e.g., ["S. Ben Jabra", "Ezzeddine Zagrouba"]). This format is not very user-friendly and could be improved. A more readable format, such as a comma-separated list, would be better (e.g., "S. Ben Jabra, Ezzeddine Zagrouba").
