Design Review - Shaik Sonia

TLDR Review :

The Simple Weather App is a user-friendly and visually appealing tool for checking weather conditions. Its minimalistic layout features a clear search bar and well-organized weather information display. The styling is modern and clean, with a pleasant color scheme and legible text. The information flow is intuitive, allowing users to quickly find weather details and recommendations for any city.

Layout

The layout is simple and straightforward.

The app features a search bar at the top where users can enter the city name.

Below the search bar, the weather information for the searched city is displayed.

The design is minimalistic, with a clear separation between the input section and the output section.

Styling

The styling is clean and modern.

The use of a blue background provides a pleasant contrast to the white content area.

The search button is prominently colored in blue, making it easily noticeable.

The weather information is presented in a card-like format, which is visually appealing and easy to read.

The text is clear and legible, with a good use of font sizes and weights.

Information Flow

The information flow is intuitive.

Users enter the city name and click the search button to get the weather details.

The weather details include the city name, temperature, weather condition (e.g., Overcast), and a brief description or recommendation based on the weather.

The flow from input to output is smooth and user-friendly.
