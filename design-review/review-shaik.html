Design Review - Shaik Sonia

TLDR Review :

One of the best information structure flow and impeccable aesthecic and modern designs I've ever seen in the exercises. The font and color choices complement each other well and the website is functional. A user does not have to do additional work in order to gain information they actually want.

Here is the review of each and every exercises along with the reason as to why
Exercise.No Topic Rating out of 10 Reason for Rating
1 Fonts 8.5

Level 1 : This is good. Went from 1.1 to 1.3 in an impeccable manner. Also provided info about font in 1.1 at the top and I didn't have to work hard for it.
Level 2 : The code has appropriate color scheming to it and the typography is good. The background color compliments the font and the header is good
Level 3: The color choice is phenomenal. Looked aesthetically and visually pleasing and the font choice was good. Margins and spacing was perfect

2 Colors 8

Level 1 : The color Scheming card exercises are one of the best and informative exercises I've seen. It gives all of the information without additional work for the user
Level 2 : The dark theme and light theme colors compliment each other well and is visually and aesthetically pleasing.
Level 3: Apart from the text inside the gradient box everything is good. Provides all of the information I need and follows a good information structure flow.

3 Icons 8.5

Level 1: One of the best implemenatations of this website I've seen. The icons are good and compliments the overall dark mode of the website. Also provided info regarding which Icons they've used in the paragraph.
Level 2: This was good but could be made better if they mentioned as to which row was SVG/ which row was Icons
Level 3: The navbar is good. All the buttons are present and have appropriate icons and have good spacing and margins when it comes to buttons.

4 Images 8.5

The Level 1 of the website was good. Provides all of the info but could be made better if additional context as to sizing was provided.

5 Layouts and Spacing 8.5

Level 1: Article was good. Has all of the contents and is following all of the principles for an article post. A user does not have to do additional work for gaining more information and the information falls into the right place at the right time
Level 2: This one is actually very good.Would've been benefitted if they added date as well so to know when the article was published

8 Design Principles and Redesign 8.5

Level 1 : This is good. Comes with Annotations which gives additional context as to why the website has bad visual choices in the first place
Level 2: The UI/UX Comparison is actually very good. The information structure followed suits well with the design choices they made.
Level 3 : The redesign has optimal spacing and font and color choices
