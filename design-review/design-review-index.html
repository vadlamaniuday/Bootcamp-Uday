The Structure I followed for reviewing the designs

As someone who gives more weightage to functionality and the ease of accessing info than aesthetics and styling, I followed the following strucutre for reviewing my peers' works :

  1. Usability: I only looked into how easy it is for users to accomplish whatever they want to achieve with the website. This includes navigation, layout, and overall user experience.
  2. Information Architecture: How the information is presented and strucutured.
  3. Performance: Looked into the loading times (in network Tab in console window).
  4. Consistency: Whether overall design is consistent in each and every page.
  5. Responsiveness:Whether the webpages are responsiveness or not.
One thing I'd like to let people know upfront is that I am bad at UI/UX (currently improving this, thanks to this bootcamp) and that my opinion might be biased since I prefer functionality over aesthetics.
